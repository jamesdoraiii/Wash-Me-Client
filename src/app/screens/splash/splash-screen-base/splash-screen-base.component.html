Frequently Asked Questions

What is automotive detailing and why would I want it?

If you have never had your vehicle proffesionally detailed then you are in for a treat. Based on what services you select a detailer will go through every nook and cranny in your car and try to make your car look showroom new. This can range from extensive interior cleaning, leather reconditioning, exterior wash, paint correction, and polishing.

 What is the pricing like?

The price depends on the amount and type of work that you want done. With Wash.me Allowing detailers to set their own prices the price you pay will depend on which detailer you contact and what services you negotiate with them.

 Who will be the one actually cleaning my car?

The detailer you contact is completely up to you. Wash Me allows you to peruse detailer profiles, see examples of their work, and the services that they offer and then hire the detailer which fits your needs.
